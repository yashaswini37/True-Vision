Features

Dynamic Background

Background adjusts to selected colors and allows saving of chosen colors.

Different Color Spaces

Displays RGB, HSV, HSL, CMYK, and HEX values for the selected color.

Upload Image

Detects and analyzes the color of the uploaded image.

CVD Simulation

Simulates color vision deficiency to show how colors appear to different vision types.

Social Sharing

Shares saved colors on social media platforms like WhatsApp,Facebook,etc.

Technologies Used :
HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Use Cases

Graphic Designers

Helps designers pick perfect color combinations for UI/UX, branding, and marketing materials.

Developers

Assists developers in selecting and converting colors between HEX, RGB, CMYK, and HSL.

People with CVD (Color Vision Deficiency)

Simulates how colors appear to individuals with different types of color blindness.

Artists & Content Creators

Helps digital artists and video editors choose accurate colors for their creative projects.

E-commerce & Product Photography

Ensures product colors match accurately across digital platforms and real-world items.
